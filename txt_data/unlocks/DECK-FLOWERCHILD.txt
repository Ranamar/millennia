   Tier 0

   DISARMAMENT

   MUSIC FESTIVAL
      Unlocks “Music Festival” Tile Improvement. 
         Generates +1 Arts XP, +1 Diplomacy XP, and +1 Ideology per nation you are in an alliance with. 1 per Nation.

   DISARMAMENT
      Unlocks “Disarmament” Bilateral Diplomatic Action. 
         Other Nation has to agree to the action
         Both Nations lose 50 Warheads and gain 50 Arts XP and reduces 50 Unrest in all Regions

   NUCLEAR CEASEFIRE
      Disables the "Nuclear Readiness" penalty for your Nation.


   Tier 2

   BURN EFFIGY
      Unlocks the “Burn Effigy” Arts Domain Power.
         Costs 50 Arts XP, 10 turn cooldown, 1.2 Progressive cost.
         Targets friendly Music Festival tile improvement.
         Fill your Culture meter to full.


   STUDENT PROTESTS
      +1 Ideology buff to University buildings.

   Tier 3

   PEACE CONVENTION
      Unlocks "Peace Convention" Diplomacy Culture Power.
         Reduces the current DEFCON level by 1.

   SECURITY COUNCIL
      Unlocks the "Invite to Security Council" Diplomatic Action.
         Disables "Nuclear Readiness" penalty for another Nation.
         Must be an Allied Nation.
         Gain +1 Innovation Per Turn for you (not for them).


   LEGACY: 
      Reach Alliance level with 50% or more of the nations within the game.

   -->
